What is Pneumonia?

Overview

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. It can be caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi.

Symptoms

  • Cough with mucus or phlegm
  • Fever, sweating, and shaking chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue and muscle pain

Causes

Common causes of pneumonia include bacterial infections like *Streptococcus pneumoniae* and viral infections like the flu.

Diagnosis

A chest X-ray is commonly used for diagnosing pneumonia. Early detection and treatment can prevent severe complications.
